What you get
When you join KU-DNA Society

If you are looking at a place where to find the best interactive learning with all the support you need, you are in the right club.

KU-DNA Lifetime Access

Whether you are a graduate, or you were part of KU-DNA Society 10 years ago, you still have access to our meetings in any place. You get to be a KU-DNA IBMer.

Get Project Files

All the source code from our interactive learning sessions will be accessible in our github repo with everyone. We propel the Open Source philosophy.

Student Membership

Once you are using the IBM DNA platform in Kenyatta University you are already a KU-DNA Society Member. All exclusive student offers will be granted to you as long as we get the badges.

100+ Courses

We do not limit our learning platform to IBM DNA platform. We have free to attend classes on campus and also free learning sites as part of the curriculum.

Expert Mentors

You get to have access to various experts in different technologies and make your idea come live.

Live Supports

Any time you feel like you need support, contact any of the Club leads and they will help you with what is bothering you.
